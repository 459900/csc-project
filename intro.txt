One day, you wake up to find yourself lying on the ground in an unfamiliar room, the smell of the rotting wooden floor overpowering almost every other scent. You look around for a bit and find what looks like a note addressed to you, informing you that you have been abducted and transported to a magical mystery mansion and now have to find (and fight) your way through traps and tricks that come your way in order to escape. It is also mentioned that there are specific rooms that might not be what they seem and could even give you unfathomable riches. Do you wish to continue?
